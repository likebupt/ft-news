## Academia / Open Research

### Post-training, Alignment, and RL
- **[D2PO: Optimizing Diffusion Samplers via Dynamic Preference](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06609)** — Reframes diffusion sampler tuning (timesteps, CFG weights) as dynamic preference optimization instead of manual heuristics.
- **[Geometric Self-Distillation for Reasoning Generalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06855)** — Uses on-policy self-distillation, with a teacher supervising the student’s own trajectories, to improve reasoning generalization.
- **[Online Data Selection Is Implicit Alignment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07023)** — Argues that online data selection during SFT can itself function as an alignment mechanism.
- **[Entropy Pacing Policy Optimization for Multi-Task Agentic Reinforcement Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07178)** — Extends LLM RL post-training to multi-task agentic settings by pacing entropy to stabilize exploration and learning.
- **[Selective Timestep Weighting and Advantage-Based Replay for Sample-Efficient Diffusion RLHF](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07693)** — Improves diffusion RLHF efficiency via informative timestep weighting and replay of high-value experience.

### Inference and Serving
- **[Trees from Marginals: Autoregressive drafting with factorized priors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06763)** — Proposes tree-structured speculative decoding with factorized draft models to produce more useful candidates per forward pass.
- **[DeLS-Spec: Decoupled Long-Short Contexts for Parallel Speculative Drafting](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07409)** — Splits long- and short-context handling to speed parallel speculative decoding for LLM inference.

### Adaptation and Vision
- **[LoCA: Spatially-Aware Low-Rank Convolutional Adaptation of Vision Foundation Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06918)** — Introduces spatially aware low-rank convolutional adapters to better preserve visual inductive biases in vision model adaptation.

### Agents, Human Interaction, and Applied LLMs
- **[Modeling Distinct Human Interaction in Web Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.17588)** — Studies how web agents can model individual user preferences and corrections during task execution.
